# Intro (Setting)

Late one evening in early spring, when the air across the Claremont Colleges campus was still warm and the San Gabriel Mountains glowed faintly under the moonlight, something unusual began to happen. The old buildings of the campus started humming with a soft electric sound, as if the ground itself had woken up. The orange groves cast long shadows across the walkways, and the wind carried whispers that sounded almost like signals. At the center of the campus, near a quiet courtyard surrounded by palm trees, a strange device had appeared overnight

She scooped freshly-ground French roast into the filter, filled the carafe with filtered water, and hit the on switch.
