# Intro (Setting)

Late one evening in early spring, when the air across the Claremont Colleges campus was still warm and the San Gabriel Mountains glowed faintly under the moonlight, something unusual began to happen. The old buildings of the campus started humming with a soft electric sound, as if the ground itself had woken up. The orange groves cast long shadows across the walkways, and the wind carried whispers that sounded almost like signals. At the center of the campus, near a quiet courtyard surrounded by palm trees, a strange device had appeared overnight

# Main Character (Named Candy)

West of College Avenue, in a lime green 1950s-era cottage, a retired CMC mathematics professor named Candy stood in her kitchen making coffee.
She scooped freshly-ground French roast into the filter, filled the machine with filtered water, and hit the on switch. Coffee began to drip into the carafe.

# Conflict or Challenge

Walking down the street after sundown, Candy started noticing a quiet, but strange sound coming from behind her back. Candy looked back, but there she saw a shadow that didn't have a real body.

# Plot Twist
The shadow lurked over Candy's shoulder and calmly stated: "Candy, I am your
father." "Please come back home, your mom misses you."
